What is this?

This is my first website! This website is my complete life. I am a 21 year old mother to two beautiful mini aussies and a very young handsome little boy. Letters is a little thing I am doing. When I got older I always wished my mother had documented more about me growing up because you forget so much. And already I have forgotten so much about Ben. I sometimes look back on pictures of him from two months ago and think I forgot how small he was. So even if he never reads them I want to try and right a letter a week about what happened, kind of a mental log for me. I want to remember as much as I can about this special time with him.
